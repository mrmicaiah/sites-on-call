About Green Matters

Since 2011, Green Matters Lawn & Landscape Management has been serving families and businesses across Hartselle, Decatur, and North Alabama with professional landscaping services built on trust, expertise, and genuine care.

Owner Daniel Owens brings a deep knowledge of plants, soil, and design — turning ordinary yards into showplaces. It's why customers have trusted us for 10, 15, even 20 years and counting.
